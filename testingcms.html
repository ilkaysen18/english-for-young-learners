Speaking With Pictures - Lesson 1 (A1–A2)

At the Beach

Vocabulary

  • sand
  • sea
  • towel
  • swim
  • hat
  • sun
  • ball
  • umbrella
  • waves
  • sunglasses

Conversation Questions

  1. Do you like going to the beach?
  2. What do you do there?
  3. Who do you go with?
  4. What do you wear at the beach?
  5. Can you swim well?

Agree or Disagree

  • The beach is the best place to relax.
  • I don’t like swimming in the sea.
  • It’s fun to play ball on the sand.
  • The sun is too hot in summer.
  • I like watching the waves.

💬 Conversation Questions

  • 1. Do you like going to the beach?
  • 2. What do you do there?
  • 3. Who do you go with?
  • 4. What do you wear at the beach?
  • 5. Can you swim well?

✅ Agree or Disagree

  • 1. The beach is the best place to relax.
  • 2. I don’t like swimming in the sea.
  • 3. It’s fun to play ball on the sand.
  • 4. The sun is too hot in summer.
  • 5. I like watching the waves.
